The largest fully-free tier with no upload cap on the page side. Drag files at [SwissTransfer](https://swisstransfer.com), generate a link, optionally add a password. 30-day retention, Swiss privacy laws, no ads, no account.

### WeTransfer (Up to 3 GB Free)

The most recognizable name. Add files at [WeTransfer](https://wetransfer.com), pick email or link, click Transfer. 7-day retention, capped at 10 transfers/month on the free tier.

### Cloud Storage for Regular Sharing

- **Google Drive (15 GB):** Share → "Anyone with the link" → paste link.
- **Dropbox (2 GB):** Share → Create link. Add `?dl=1` for direct download.
- **OneDrive (5 GB):** Share → copy link.

Use these when you need the file to stay around long-term.

## FAQ

**What's the largest file I can send for free?**
Unlimited with Transfer.zip Quick Transfer (P2P). Otherwise SwissTransfer's 50 GB is the highest single-transfer cap.

**Do I need to create an account?**
No — Transfer.zip, SwissTransfer, and WeTransfer all work without one. Cloud storage (Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive) requires sign-up.

**Are free services safe for sensitive files?**
Yes, the reputable ones. For private files, pick a service with end-to-end encryption (Transfer.zip) and set a password.
